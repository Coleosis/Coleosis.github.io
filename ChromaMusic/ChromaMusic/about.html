Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a service charge?

Our creators main income is service charges, so we charge an automatic 20% gratuity.

What kind of instruments do you make?

We currently create custom acoustic and electric forms of Guitar, Bass, Ukelele, and Mandolin.

What happens if I cancel my order?

Once an order is placed it is final. If you cancel an order once work has begun, a cancelation fee of $200 will be charged to the credit card on file.
