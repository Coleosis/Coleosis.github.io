    Frequently Asked Questions

    Is there a service charge?

    It is commonplace to include a tip to the creator, so we charge an automatic 20% gratuity.

    What instruments do you make?

    We currently create custom acoustic and electric versions of:

    • Guitars
    • Ukeleles
    • Bass'

    What happens if I cancel my order?

    Once an order is placed it is final. If you cancel an order once work has started, a cancelation fee of $200 will be charged to the credit card on file.
